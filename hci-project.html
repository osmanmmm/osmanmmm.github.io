Project Description

CampusCare is a centralized platform that consolidates mental health resources for Carleton students. It simplifies access to wellness tools, reduces misinformation, and provides a peer support space. The platform integrates events, therapist information, and student groups to improve mental well-being at Carleton College.

How our Prototype Meets User Needs

The CampusCare prototype is designed to provide easy access to mental health resources through a structured homepage, a calendar of wellness events, and a peer support section. The interface prioritizes clarity, with recommendations based on student blogs and reviews, ensuring every user finds relevant support.
